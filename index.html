Hi I'm Vihanga, Nice to Meet you

I'm a UI/UX designer specializing in enterprise interfaces and scalable design systems. I design thoughtful, user-centered experiences that help businesses run smoother and connect better with their users.

Since the start of my career at Cam Management Solutions, I've worked remotely with teams across the UK, Norway, and Sri Lanka. From fast-paced startups to growing SaaS platforms, I’ve helped shape design strategies that align with both business goals and user needs.

What sets me apart? I bring structure to complexity. Whether I’m building a design system from scratch or simplifying user journeys in enterprise tools, my focus is always on creating intuitive, consistent, and scalable solutions.

Outside of work, I’m passionate about personal growth and creative learning. I love experimenting with new tech, journaling my ideas, and even dreaming up business concepts I might launch one day. I also enjoy practicing karate, working out at home, and learning how to cook healthy meals—especially ones that support my fitness goals.

I believe great design goes beyond screens. It’s about empathy, systems thinking, and crafting solutions that feel human.
